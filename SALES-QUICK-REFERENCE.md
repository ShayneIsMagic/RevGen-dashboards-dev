# Quick Reference Guide - Sales & Marketing Tracking

## IMPROV Stage Quick Reference

| Stage | Focus | Key Event | Typical Duration | Success Rate |
|-------|-------|-----------|------------------|--------------|
| **I**ntro | Initial contact & qualification | Introductory call | 2-3 days | 85% advance |
| **M**utual Discovery | Understand needs & fit | Discovery meeting | 7-10 days | 78% advance |
| **P**roposal | Present solution | Proposal presentation | 10-15 days | 72% advance or close |
| **R**ealign | Adjust scope/budget | Realignment meeting | 8-12 days | 90% advance |
| **O**vercome | Address objections | Final questions session | 12-18 days | 88% close |
| **V**isit | Relationship maintenance & referral generation | Client visits, QBRs, check-ins | Ongoing (4+ per year) | Referral generation |

**Can Close At**: Proposal (25%), Realign (15%), Overcome (60%)

**Visit Stage**: The Visit stage is for active and former clients. It focuses on maintaining relationships, collecting testimonials and success stories, and generating referrals. This stage feeds back into the Intro stage when referrals are received.

### Visit Stage Details

- **Purpose**: Maintain client relationships, collect marketing assets, generate referrals
- **Frequency**: Minimum 4 visits per client per year
- **Visit Types**:
  - Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs)
  - Check-in meetings
  - Project kickoffs
  - Problem-solving sessions
  - Celebration meetings
- **Key Outcomes**:
  - Testimonials collected (written/video)
  - Success stories with metrics
  - Referrals received
  - Client health score updates
  - Upsell opportunities identified
- **Success Metrics**:
  - 50%+ of visits generate testimonials
  - 2+ referrals per client per year
  - Client health score >85
  - Testimonial collection rate >50%

## Data Entry Checklist

### When You Get a New Lead
- [ ] Enter lead information (name, company, contact)
- [ ] Record source (referral/networking/testimonial/association)
- [ ] If referral: Record referrer client ID
- [ ] If networking: Record event name and date
- [ ] If testimonial: Record platform they found you on
- [ ] Assign to sales person
- [ ] Set initial qualification meeting
- [ ] Calculate lead score

### When You Schedule a Sales Event
- [ ] Record event type
- [ ] Record SET date (when you scheduled it)
- [ ] Record SCHEDULED FOR date (when it will happen)
- [ ] Calculate days between set and scheduled
- [ ] Note anticipated next stage
- [ ] Add to calendar
- [ ] Send confirmation

### After a Sales Event
- [ ] Record completed date
- [ ] Record actual next stage
- [ ] Compare anticipated vs actual
- [ ] Record outcome (positive/negative/needs-adjustment)
- [ ] Log key discussion points
- [ ] Schedule next event (if applicable)
- [ ] Update opportunity probability
- [ ] Move to appropriate stage

### After a Client Visit (Visit Stage)
- [ ] Record visit details (type, date, attendees)
- [ ] Log topics discussed and outcomes
- [ ] Note satisfaction level
- [ ] Record any testimonials collected
- [ ] Record any stories collected
- [ ] Record any referrals received
- [ ] Update client health score
- [ ] Schedule next visit
- [ ] Link referrals to new leads

### When Collecting a Testimonial (Visit Stage)
- [ ] Record collection date and method
- [ ] Note who collected it
- [ ] Link to originating visit
- [ ] Record testimonial content
- [ ] Note rating (1-5 stars)
- [ ] Confirm permission to publish
- [ ] Record where published
- [ ] Flag if used in marketing

### When Receiving a Referral (Visit Stage)
- [ ] Record referring client
- [ ] Note when/how received
- [ ] Link to originating visit
- [ ] Enter referral contact details
- [ ] Create new lead record
- [ ] Record estimated value
- [ ] Note referral incentive offered
- [ ] Contact within 24 hours
- [ ] Update referral status

## Visit Stage Best Practices

### Visit Frequency
- **Minimum**: 4 visits per client per year
- **Ideal**: Quarterly Business Reviews + ad-hoc check-ins
- **Alert**: Flag clients with >90 days since last visit

### Visit Types & Goals
1. **Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs)**
   - Review performance metrics
   - Discuss upcoming projects
   - Identify upsell opportunities
   - Collect testimonials and stories

2. **Check-in Meetings**
   - Quick status updates
   - Address concerns early
   - Maintain relationship warmth
   - Opportunity for referrals

3. **Project Kickoffs**
   - Set expectations
   - Build excitement
   - Collect initial feedback
   - Document success metrics baseline

4. **Problem-Solving Sessions**
   - Address issues quickly
   - Demonstrate value
   - Turn challenges into success stories
   - Maintain trust

5. **Celebration Meetings**
   - Recognize achievements
   - Collect testimonials
   - Request referrals
   - Document success metrics

### Visit Outcomes Tracking
Every visit should track:
- Satisfaction rating (1-5)
- Testimonials collected (count and type)
- Stories collected (count and type)
- Referrals received (count and details)
- Next steps identified
- Health score impact
